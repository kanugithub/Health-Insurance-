What is Health Insurance?

Health insurance is a type of insurance that covers the cost of medical and surgical expenses. It can be provided by your employer, purchased individually, or provided by the government. The goal of health insurance is to protect you from financial ruin in case of unexpected medical expenses.

Why Do I Need Health Insurance?

Health insurance is important because it can help you pay for medical expenses that can be costly. Health insurance can also provide access to preventive care, which can help you stay healthy and catch medical conditions early. Without health insurance, you may be at risk of medical debt or bankruptcy if you get sick or injured.
